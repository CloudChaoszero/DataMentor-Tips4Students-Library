If the *JSON Viewer chrome extension* is not working for your geoJSON data, then you can try two things:




1. change the settings of `maxSize` to something larger than the defaulted value. Your size of dat amay be too large.


2. Close and reopen Google Chrome



Source:
https://github.com/tulios/json-viewer/issues/60
